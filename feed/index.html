The rays radiating from Krishna illuminates everything. From Krishna comes His expansion Maha Vishnu, who lies in the Causal Ocean. While lying in this ocean, innumerable material universes emanate from the pores of Maha Vishnu’s body. From every pore of His body, one universe comes out by His sweet desire. The body of intelligence enjoys the objects of sense gratification that cover it, such as smell, vision and hearing. The word sunāsām, “beautiful nose,” indicates the organ for acquiring knowledge by smell.
