GITA CHAMPIONS LEAGUE | IITians giving Gita contest on ZOOM and Kahoot | Contest 1 | Bhagwad Geeta

GITA CHAMPIONS LEAGUE is an online quiz contest based on Bhagavad Gita. GITA CHAMPIONS LEAGUE gives a creative interface to access unparalleled wisdom of Bhagavad Gita and apply it in one’s life This is the world’s first Gita contest held online where 27 IITians participated and was conducted under the guidance of Prof. Laxmidhar Behera and Prof Vipul Arora
